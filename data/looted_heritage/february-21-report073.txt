Archaeological Sites Looted in Syria

Hama Antiquities Directorate revealed secret excavations, random digging and steal operations of mosaic paintings and archeological treasures in the archeological city of Apamia.

Head of Apamia Antiquities Department, Abdul Qader Farzat, said that a number of photos taken with a cell phone will be documented to inform the authorities concerned so they will chase the thieves.

He added that the stolen thing included an important mosaic painting of waterwheels and other artistic shapes.

Farzat said that the archeological sites which have been attacked are Kherbet Um Keibat and other sites in Zayzoun, Qastoun, Tar al-Ola, Qanas and tamak.
